Privacy Policy

Bedtime Stories: Picture Books

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Bedtime Stories: Picture Books ("the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Reading preferences (font size, theme, line spacing)
  • Reading progress and bookmarks
  • Favorite stories

This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

  • Collects device advertising ID, IP address, and general location
  • Used to display advertisements within the App
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

GitHub Pages (Content Delivery)

  • Story content is downloaded from GitHub Pages
  • Standard server logs may record IP address and request data
  • GitHub Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Only the third-party services listed above may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

All App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by clearing the App data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

6. Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

7. Security

We value your trust. Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this policy.
